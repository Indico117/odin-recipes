How to make the best Hamburger

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground Beef: Feel free to use ground beef with a bit of fat for more flavour
  • Hamburger Buns: Something to hold our delicious creation together that isn't plain bread
  • Onion: Flavour enhancement
  • Tomato: The perfect addition to make the perfect burger
  • Sauce of Choice: In the end a perfect burger is up to the person eating it, so choose your favourite sauce

Making the Burger

  1. Create balls of the ground beef and on the pan press it down to create the burger shape
  2. Cook the burger to your desired rarity
  3. I shouldn't have to tell you how to put together the rest
  4. Enjoy!!

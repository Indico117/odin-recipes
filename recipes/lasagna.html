How to make Lasagna

Making lasagna may take quite a bit of time, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Paste and Tomato sauce to add that delicious saucy taste
  • Sugar to make it sweet
  • Cheese of personal choice

Steps to take

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna and bake

How to make Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is by far the best recipe I have found to make the perfect Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients

  • Butter: The staple to making a good cookie is good butter
  • Eggs: Unfortunately if you aren't able to eat eggs you can't eat perfection
  • Vailla: The best way to add that extra flavour
  • Baking Soda: Nobody wants a flat, lifeless cookie, this will add that fluffy look
  • Water: Similar to the baking soda this will give it some fluff
  • Salt: No this won't make the cookie have a salty taste
  • Flour: Did you think you can make a cookie without this? You were right but we are using it anyway
  • Chocolate Chips: The "Chocolate Chip" part of the cookie

Step by Step Baking Process

  1. Beat the Butter and Sugars, then beat in the Eggs and Vanilla into one bowl
  2. Dissolve the Baking Soda in hot Water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the Flour, Chocolate Chips
  4. Drop the dough onto a baking sheet
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown
